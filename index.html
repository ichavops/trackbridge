How It Works

Three steps.
Seconds to an answer.

TrackBridge connects directly to your PSA, interprets your live operational data, and delivers plain-English intelligence wherever your team works.

1

Connect Your PSA

Link TrackBridge to your existing PSA platform via secure API. No data migration. No parallel systems. Your data stays exactly where it is — we just make it accessible.

2

Ask a Question

Type your question in plain English — in the TrackBridge web app, in Slack, or in Microsoft Teams. No dashboards to navigate. No filters to configure.

3

Get Your Answer

TrackBridge retrieves the relevant live data from your PSA and returns a precise, context-aware response in seconds — with the numbers, the context, and what to do about it.

A note on data accuracy

TrackBridge never calculates core metrics itself. Your PSA platform computes utilization, budget consumption, and project financials using your existing business rules and configurations. TrackBridge retrieves those figures and explains what they mean, what changed, and what to watch. This architecture ensures full data fidelity and maintains trust with finance and operations leadership — the numbers you see are your PSA's numbers, not an approximation.
